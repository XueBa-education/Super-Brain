舒尔特方格 Schulte Grid

舒尔特方格是一种注意力训练方法。游戏规则：按数字顺序点击方格中的数字，从1开始依次点击，直到点击完所有数字。 完成后会显示你的用时，用时越短说明注意力越集中。
The Schulte Grid is an attention training method. Game rules: Click the numbers in the grid in order from 1 to the highest number. Your completion time will be displayed at the end. Shorter times indicate better concentration.

记忆扑克牌 Memory Cards

记忆扑克牌游戏可以训练你的短期记忆能力。游戏规则：点击翻开两张牌，如果图案相同则保持翻开状态， 如果不同则会翻回去。记住牌的位置，找出所有相同的牌对。
The Memory Card game trains your short-term memory. Game rules: Flip two cards at a time. If they match, they stay face-up. If not, they flip back. Remember card positions to find all matching pairs.

反应力游戏 Reaction Test

反应力游戏测试你的反应速度和注意力。游戏规则：5张卡片会在方形格子中快速移动，其中一张会变成绿色。 你需要尽快点击绿色的卡片才能得分。游戏时间为1分钟，点击正确的绿色卡片越多，得分越高。
The Reaction Test measures your reaction speed and attention. Game rules: 5 cards will move quickly in a square grid, with one turning green. Click the green card as quickly as possible to score. The game lasts 1 minute - more correct clicks mean higher scores.

斯特鲁普效应实验 Stroop Effect

斯特鲁普效应展示了当文字含义与文字颜色不一致时，人们的反应会变慢。游戏规则：忽略文字本身的含义， 选择文字实际显示的颜色。完成1分钟内尽可能多的测试，会显示你在一致和不一致条件下的平均反应时间。
The Stroop Effect demonstrates slower reaction times when word meaning and color don't match. Game rules: Ignore the word's meaning, select the actual color it's displayed in. Complete as many tests as possible in 1 minute to see your average times.
